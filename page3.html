WFRD Vehicles (Scope 1) Calculations based on Distance (Miles/Kilometers):

Note:

This calculator is for internal (Weatherford employees) use only.

Methodology/Disclaimers:

Emission Factors utilized are from the US EPA's GHG Emission Factors Hub The factors are based on vehicle type (passenger car, light-duty truck, motorcycle, medium-duty and heavy-duty truck.

Please refer to the Weatherford Emissions Calculator Methodology page for a more detailed methodology on how the emissions are calculated including references/links to utilized emission factors.

This calculator does not provide an output obtained through formal LCA (Life Cycle Analysis), if you need an LCA conducted, please contact the Sustainability Team.

Guidance:

To calculate emissions, simply enter distance in kilometers or miles for the applicable vehicle types.

Note:

Methodology/Disclaimers:
